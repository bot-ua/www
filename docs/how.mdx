Bot harnesses the reasoning capabilities of Large Language Models (LLMs) to automate workflows, tasks, and scheduling. Users can customize 'Skills', ensuring a proactive alignment with their routines and preferences. The flexibility to run on personal hardware or offload to the cloud offers unparalleled convenience. To understand the intricate details of our approach, please consult our [whitepaper](https://github.com/bot-ua/whitepaper/blob/main/WHITEPAPER.pdf).
